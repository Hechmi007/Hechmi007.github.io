About OPEX

One expert.
Complete focus.

OPEX for Management and Consulting is built on a single conviction: the best consulting is personal. When you engage OPEX, you work with a senior consultant throughout — not a rotating cast of analysts managed from a distance.

Decades of hands-on experience across industrial manufacturing, railway infrastructure, and automotive supply chains means the perspective brought to each engagement is genuinely cross-sector. Problems that seem intractable in one industry often have elegant precedents in another.
